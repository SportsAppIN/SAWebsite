    ‘Protsahan’

    Money can’t buy Medals, Let our athletes earn them.

    Help them on the way!

    SportsApp urges your support in our crowdfunding initiative so that our sporting heroes carry on the dignity they serve our nation with. Help them realize their dreams without any tensions, any worries. Together we can promote sports and brilliance in the country. A small contribution from your end can work wonders for the sports fraternity of our country

    Mission

    To support athletes going to big sporting events like Olympics, Paralympics, Commonwealth Games, Asian Games etc.

    Vision

    To build a nurturing ecosystem for athletes, where financial constraints hindering their progress, are nullified.

    Why we are doing this

    In India, there remains a huge asymmetry in the way athletes are treated across various sports. Through this initiative of ours, we would like to inculcate equality for all the athletes, while providing a platform for true sports lovers to show their support and contribution towards them.

    We will ensure that your support reaches athletes

    SportsApp will charge 10% on the amount raised upto ₹ 5,00,000 and 5% on above ₹ 5,00,000

    Our Athletes

    These worthy flag bearers of Indian sports, work hard to make us proud. They have won several accolades for the country at various tournaments and competitions, to bring glory to our great nation.
